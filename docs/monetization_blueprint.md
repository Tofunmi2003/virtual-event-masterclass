# Event Monetization Blueprint (Platinum Deliverable)

## Phase 1: Pre-Event Setup
*   **The "Perfect Promise" Worksheet**: Defining the hook that gets signups.
*   **Tech Stack Setup**:
    *   Systeme.io (Landing Pages & Email)
    *   Zoom (Delivery)
    *   Stripe (Payments)
*   **Asset Checklist**: Slides, Workbooks, Email Copy.

## Phase 2: The Traffic Engine
*   **Organic Promo Schedule**: 14-Day Social Media Calendar.
*   **Partner/Affiliate Swipe Copy**: Templates to get others to promote you.
*   **The "Show Up" Sequence**: 3 Emails to ensure registrants actually attend.

## Phase 3: The Run-of-Show (Day by Day)
*   **Day 1 Script Structure**: Establishing Authority & The Problem.
*   **Day 2 Script Structure**: The Solution & The Soft Pivot.
*   **Day 3 Script Structure**: The Offer & The Close.
*   **Transitions**: Exactly what to say to move from teaching to selling.

## Phase 4: Post-Event Monetization
*   **The Replay Strategy**: How to sell the recordings.
*   **The Downsell**: Monetizing the "No's".
*   **Evergreen Transition**: Turning the live recording into an automated funnel.
